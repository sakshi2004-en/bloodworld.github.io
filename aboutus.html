About Us

We are dedicated to saving lives by organizing blood donation drives and spreading awareness.

Our Mission

To create a reliable network of blood donors and ensure timely availability of safe blood for those in need.

Why Donate Blood?

Donating blood saves lives and promotes a healthier community. Your one donation can help multiple people.

Who Can Donate?

  • Healthy individuals aged 18-65
  • Minimum weight of 50 kg
  • No recent major surgeries
  • Free from infectious diseases

How to Donate?

Locate a blood donation camp, complete a quick health screening, and donate to save lives!
